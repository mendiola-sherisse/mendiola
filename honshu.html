✧ Honshu, Japan ✧

Why It's Special

Honshu represents the perfect blend of traditional Japanese culture and modern innovation. From ancient temples to bustling cities, it offers an incredible journey through Japan's past and present.

How to Get There

  • Fly to Tokyo's Narita (NRT) or Haneda (HND) Airport
  • Use Japan Rail Pass for inter-city travel
  • Local transport: trains, buses, and metros
  • Best time to visit: Spring (March-May) or Fall (September-November)

Must-Visit Places

  • Mount Fuji
  • Kyoto's Historic Temples
  • Tokyo Skytree
  • Osaka Castle
  • Hiroshima Peace Memorial
  • Nikko National Park
