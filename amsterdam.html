✧ Amsterdam, Netherlands ✧

Why It's Special

Amsterdam captures my heart with its picturesque canals, historic architecture, and vibrant culture. The city's blend of old-world charm and modern innovation creates a unique atmosphere I'd love to experience.

How to Get There

  • Fly to Amsterdam Airport Schiphol (AMS)
  • Take a direct train from the airport to Amsterdam Central Station
  • Public transport options: trams, buses, and metros
  • Best time to visit: April-May (tulip season) or September

Must-Visit Places

  • Anne Frank House
  • Van Gogh Museum
  • Vondelpark
  • Royal Palace
  • Keukenhof Gardens (during tulip season)
  • Canal Ring (UNESCO World Heritage site)
